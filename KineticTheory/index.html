1. Constant Random Motion

Notice how particles move in perfectly straight lines until they hit a wall or another particle, creating an endless, chaotic dance.

2. What is Pressure?

Pressure isn't a magical force; it's the combined physical impact of millions of tiny collisions against the container walls.

3. Temperature = Speed

Increase the temperature. The particles don't grow; they just move much faster. Higher Kinetic Energy directly equals higher Temperature.

4. Boyle's & Charles's Laws

Shrink the volume (Boyle's Law) or raise the temp (Gay-Lussac's Law). Watch the pressure gauge spike as wall hits become more frequent/violent.
